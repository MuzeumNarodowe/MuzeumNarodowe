Name of the museum: "National Museum" Address: "Al. Jerozolimskie 3, Lublin, Poland" Opening hours: "Open Tuesday through Sunday from 10:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m."
Ticket prices: "A regular ticket costs PLN 20, a discounted ticket costs PLN 15, a family ticket costs PLN 40."
Description of the collection: "The National Museum contains collections of Polish and European art from the Middle Ages to the present day, including paintings, sculptures, graphics and archaeological relics."
Accessibility: "The museum is accessible to people with disabilities, wheelchairs are available"
Tour duration: "The tour lasts about 2-3 hours"
Tour start date and time: "The tour will start on Sunday, January 31 at 10:00 a.m."
Transportation: "Transportation to the museum is provided by bus, departure from the railroad station at 9:00 am"
Guide: "The tour will be led by a licensed guide who will talk about the most important works and history of the museum."
